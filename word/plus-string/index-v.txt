title: +STRING
status:
 standardized in OTA (Open Terminal Architecture)
description:

 +STRING  ( c-addr1 len1 c-addr2 len2 - c-addr2 len3 )
    Append the string at c-addr1, for len1 bytes, to the end
    of the string at c-addr2, for len2 bytes.  Return the
    beginning of the destination string (c-addr2) and the
    sum of the two lengths (len3).  It is the programmer's
    responsibility to ensure that there is room at the end
    of the destination string to hold both strings.
