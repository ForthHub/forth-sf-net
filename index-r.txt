
Welcome to the comp.lang.forth.repository.

This site is provided as a webspace to be
used on usenet' comp.lang.forth. It is
based on the obvervation, that every once
in a while someone creates a webpage with
proposals, informations, examples,
forthtexts or just hypertext links.

A lot of these private webpages are
scattered around the web, and it is
often not easy to find them unless
they are mentioned again on comp.lang.forth.
That does not make life easy for a developer
who wishes to get an overview of what people
are doing out there with forth, not to speak
of someone who wishes to present interesting
ideas that may otherwise soon be forgotten
just because no one can find them anymore.

The advantage of sourceforge is that
everyone can get a developer account
here. If you want to have write access
to this repository, just go and make
yourself heard on the usenet' group.
They will perhaps ask you a few questions
to be sure that you know quite a bit
about forth, and an instant thereafter
you can do with these files what you want.

And remember, it is harder to find someone
who actually *wants* to have write access
than there would be a need to be wary about
who is applying for the job. Just give it
a try, the people on comp.lang.forth can
not hurt you (atleast physically, heehee).
