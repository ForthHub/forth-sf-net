Collections of words that can
be grouped together under a
descriptive name. In most cases
they are not under a specific
functional category, instead they
bear the names of the project
they were the underlying set
of basic words.

It is assumed that there could
be compatibility extensions in a
number of forth systems that
could be loaded at runtime to
provide the specified behaviour,
i.e. using INCLUDED or NEEDED.

People having a set of favourite
words that they use in more than
one project should really consider
to place their wordset here for
reference. May be implementors
of other forth system will like
your ideas and implement your
wordset in full so that your
scripts may become even portable.
